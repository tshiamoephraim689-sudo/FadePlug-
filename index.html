The FadePlug Story

Welcome to FadePlug, where precision meets style. Established in 2021, we have quickly become the go-to destination for gentlemen who value a sharp look and a classic barbershop experience.

Owned and operated by Lethu, our shop combines old-school barbering traditions with modern trends. Whether you need a quick fade, a straight razor shave, or a full hair treatment, our team is dedicated to making you look and feel your absolute best.

At FadePlug, we don't just cut hair—we craft confidence.

- Lethu, Owner
